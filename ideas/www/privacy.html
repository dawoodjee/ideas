Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to IDEAS, a Notion-based productivity app designed to help you capture your best ideas and improve focus. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use IDEAS.

By using IDEAS, you agree to the terms outlined below.

2. What Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide you with a seamless experience:

  • Personal Information: Name and email when you purchase or sign up for IDEAS.
  • Payment Data: Transaction details (e.g., amount, date) processed securely via our payment partner, Take.app, Network, Lenco, or any other provider.
  • Technical Data: IP address, device information, and usage data (e.g., page views, clicks, scrolls) for security and analytics purposes via Google Analytics 4 and Microsoft Clarity.

We do not access or store the content of your ideas entered into Notion—those remain private within your Notion account.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Process your purchase and provide access to IDEAS.
  • Send you updates, receipts, or community-related information (if opted in).
  • Improve IDEAS and enhance user experience.
  • Ensure security and comply with legal requirements.

4. How We Protect Your Data

Your security matters to us. We use encryption and industry-standard practices to safeguard your data during transmission. Note that your idea content is stored in Notion, and its security is governed by Notion’s Privacy Policy.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Take.app, Lenco, DPO: Our payment processor(s), to complete transactions.
  • Google Analytics 4: To analyze website usage and improve user experience (see Google’s Privacy Policy).
  • Microsoft Clarity: To understand user behavior via heatmaps and session recordings (see Microsoft’s Privacy Statement).
  • Regulatory authorities: If required by law.

6. Your Rights & Choices

You can:

  • Request to see the data we have about you.
  • Ask us to delete your personal information (except where required by law).
  • Opt out of email updates anytime via the unsubscribe link.

7. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies (e.g., via Google Analytics 4 and Microsoft Clarity) on our website to enhance your browsing experience and analyze usage patterns. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may limit analytics functionality and affect some features.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. Changes will be posted here, so please check back periodically.

9. Contact Us
